Welcome to Q Condos Your Future Begins Here

Your Next Chapter Starts Here

Welcome to Q Condos, the newest landmark in Downtown Kitchener, soaring 34 storeys above Queen Street’s creative hub. Designed to complement the city’s energy, Q Condos offers the perfect blend of modern design, smart technology, and urban convenience—all just steps from Victoria Park, ION rapid transit, top restaurants, entertainment, and the region’s thriving tech scene.

Built with Momentum, Designed for the Future

Q Condos joins the prestigious lineup of Downtown Kitchener and Uptown Waterloo developments by Momentum Developments, including Charlie West, Garment Street, One Victoria, and One Hundred Condominiums. For nearly two decades, Momentum Developments has been shaping the skyline of Waterloo Region, crafting thriving communities with exceptional quality, modern design, and a vision for the future.

OUR Projects

At Momentum Developments, we create more than just buildings—we design spaces that inspire, connect, and enhance everyday life. Our projects are built with a commitment to quality, innovation, and community, ensuring a seamless blend of modern living and timeless design.

The One Hundred Towers One Hundred Condominiums kickstarted the Garment Street urban village. Now, two towers stand tall, overlooking the Innovation District, alongside Garment Street Condos.
Garment Street Condos When Garment Street Condos was introduced, the urban village began to take shape, cradling the Glovebox corporate centre to complete this multi-use development.
The Glovebox Office Centre The Huck Glove factory was restored with its original wood beams and brick, complemented by modern glass office expansions.
